Our Services

Comprehensive sign solutions for your business

Sign Installation

Our professional team handles all aspects of sign installation, from small interior signs to large exterior displays. We ensure proper mounting, electrical connections, and structural integrity for all installations.

At Florida Sign Solution, we understand that your signage is often the first impression customers have of your business. That's why our expert installation team approaches each project with meticulous attention to detail and unwavering commitment to quality. Using industry-leading techniques and premium-grade materials, we ensure your signs not only look spectacular but also withstand Florida's challenging weather conditions for years to come.

Our comprehensive installation process includes site assessment, structural analysis, precise mounting, secure fastening, electrical wiring, and final quality inspection. We coordinate all aspects of the installation, including scheduling, equipment, and safety protocols, allowing you to focus on your business while we handle the complexities of bringing your signage vision to life.

Our Installation Services Include:
  • Channel Letter Installation: Precision mounting of illuminated and non-illuminated dimensional letters for maximum visibility and brand impact
  • Monument Sign Installation: Ground-level signage installation with proper foundation work and landscaping integration
  • Pylon Sign Installation: Tall, freestanding sign structures engineered for stability and high visibility from roadways
  • Wall Sign Mounting: Secure attachment to various building materials with minimal structural impact
  • LED Sign Installation: Expert electrical work and programming of dynamic digital displays

Sign Maintenance & Repair

Keep your signage looking its best with our comprehensive maintenance and repair services. We handle everything from LED replacements to structural repairs and regular cleaning.

Your signage represents a significant investment in your brand's visibility and public image. At Florida Sign Solution, we're committed to protecting that investment through our comprehensive maintenance and repair services. A well-maintained sign not only preserves your professional image but also extends the lifespan of your signage, maximizing your return on investment.

Our experienced technicians are equipped to diagnose and resolve even the most complex signage issues. Whether it's weather damage, electrical failures, vandalism repair, or simply the effects of time, our team responds promptly with the right tools and expertise. We offer both emergency repair services and scheduled maintenance programs tailored to your specific signage needs and budget.

Our Maintenance & Repair Services Include:
  • LED and Lighting Repairs: Replacement of burnt-out bulbs, LED modules, and transformers to restore optimal illumination and visibility
  • Sign Face Replacements: Renewal of damaged, faded, or outdated sign faces with durable, weather-resistant materials
  • Structural Repairs: Reinforcement and restoration of sign structures affected by weather, accidents, or age-related deterioration
  • Electrical Troubleshooting: Comprehensive diagnostics and repair of complex electrical issues, including wiring, connections, and control systems
  • Regular Maintenance Programs: Customized preventative care schedules including cleaning, inspection, and minor repairs to prevent costly failures

Sign Permitting

Navigating local sign codes and permitting requirements can be challenging. Our team handles the entire permitting process, ensuring your signage meets all local regulations and requirements.

The permitting process is often the most complex and time-consuming aspect of any signage project. Florida's municipalities each have their own unique codes, zoning requirements, and approval processes that can delay or even derail your signage plans. At Florida Sign Solution, we transform this potential obstacle into a seamless experience through our specialized permitting expertise.

Our dedicated permitting specialists maintain up-to-date knowledge of local regulations across Florida and have established professional relationships with permitting officials in numerous jurisdictions. This insider knowledge allows us to anticipate potential issues before they arise and navigate the approval process efficiently. By entrusting us with your permitting needs, you can avoid costly delays, rejected applications, and the frustration of dealing with complex bureaucratic processes.

Our Permitting Services Include:
  • Code Compliance Review: Thorough analysis of your signage plans against local regulations to identify and address potential compliance issues early
  • Permit Application Preparation: Expert compilation and submission of all required documentation, including engineered drawings, structural calculations, and electrical plans
  • Variance Requests: Strategic preparation and presentation of variance applications when your ideal signage requires exceptions to existing codes
  • Zoning Compliance: Navigation of complex zoning requirements, including size restrictions, height limitations, and placement regulations
  • Inspection Coordination: Scheduling and management of all required inspections, ensuring your project meets all safety and compliance standards

Graphic Design

Our talented design team creates eye-catching, effective signage that communicates your brand message. From concept to final design, we work closely with you to develop signage that stands out.

Exceptional signage begins with exceptional design. At Florida Sign Solution, our award-winning design team combines artistic creativity with strategic marketing principles to create signage that doesn't just attract attention—it converts viewers into customers.
