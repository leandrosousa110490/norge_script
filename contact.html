Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How much does a sign typically cost?

Sign costs vary widely based on size, materials, complexity, and whether it's illuminated. We provide free quotes tailored to your specific requirements. Contact us with your project details for an accurate estimate.

How long does it take to complete a sign project?

Timeline depends on project complexity. Simple signs may take 1-2 weeks, while larger custom projects can take 4-8 weeks from design approval to installation. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the consultation process.

Do you handle permitting for sign installation?

Yes, we manage the entire permitting process. Our team is familiar with sign codes and regulations throughout Florida, and we'll handle all the paperwork and submissions to ensure your sign meets local requirements.
