About Florida Sign Solution

A father-son legacy of excellence in signage solutions across Florida.

Founded in 2005 by Norge Martinez, Florida Sign Solution began as a small workshop in Miami with a vision to provide exceptional quality signage with personalized service. What started as a one-man operation quickly grew into a respected family business when Norge's son, Alejandro Martinez, joined the company in 2023.

Today, our father-son team combines Norge's 30+ years of industry experience with Alejandro's innovative design approach and technical expertise. This powerful partnership allows us to honor traditional craftsmanship while embracing cutting-edge technology and modern design principles. The Martinez family's hands-on approach ensures that every project receives personal attention from initial consultation through final installation.

As a true family business, we understand the importance of trust, integrity, and long-term relationships. Our clients aren't just customers â€“ they become part of our extended family. This philosophy has helped us grow from a local Miami business to one of the most trusted sign companies in Florida, now serving clients across all major cities in the state.

With our state-of-the-art facility and dedicated team of professionals, we ensure your signage needs are met with the same care and attention we would give to our own business. Our commitment to excellence has earned us a reputation for delivering high-quality signage solutions that help businesses stand out and make a lasting impression.
