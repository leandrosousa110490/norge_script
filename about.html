About Florida Sign Solution

Your trusted sign partner since 1995

Our Story

For over 25 years, Florida Sign Solution has been a leading provider of high-quality sign services throughout Florida. Starting as a small family business in Miami, we've grown into a full-service sign company with clients across the state, while maintaining our commitment to personal service and exceptional quality.

Our founder, Michael Johnson, began the company with a simple vision: to create attractive, effective signage that helps businesses succeed. That vision continues to guide us today as we leverage the latest technology and materials to deliver innovative sign solutions.
